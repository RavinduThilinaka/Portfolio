Food Site

It includes features like product categorization, a streamlined checkout process, and a responsive design for accessibility across all devices. Performance optimization ensures fast loading speeds, enhancing user satisfaction.

Star Light E-Commerce

Star Light is an interactive e-commerce platform focused on delivering a seamless shopping experience. The website features an elegant and modern design, allowing users to browse, search, and purchase products effortlessly.

It integrates secure payment gateways, a user-friendly dashboard, and smart filtering options to help users find their desired products quickly. The responsive layout ensures an optimized experience across desktops and mobile devices.

Tea Factory

The Tea Factory website simplifies tea sales and business management. It offers a well-organized interface, allowing users to explore different tea varieties and place bulk orders with ease.

The platform includes supplier dashboards for inventory management, real-time order tracking, and an efficient purchasing process. With a focus on usability and performance, the site ensures a seamless experience for both business owners and customers.
